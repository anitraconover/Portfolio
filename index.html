About

I am a GIS Analyst and MGIS graduate student who loves solving real-world problems with spatial data. I’m especially drawn to projects that blend environmental insight, spatial data science, and clear, intuitive cartography. Across all my work, I’m driven by clear communication, thoughtful cartography, and building tools that help people understand and make decisions about the landscapes around them.

I currently work as a GIS Analyst at Apex Engineering Group supporting municipal infrastructure projects. My responsibilies include mapping water and sewer systems, enhancing field work with Survey123, building Experience Builder tools, and helping cities manage their assets more effectively. I enjoy the mix of technical problem-solving, teamwork, and creating maps that actually get used in daily operations.
