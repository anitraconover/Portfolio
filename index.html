Projects

Environmental Modeling

Sweet Sorghum Suitability Web App

Research • 2025

This project models sweet sorghum production across Northwest Minnesota using Daymet climate data, soil characteristics, and land cover. I processed large climate rasters, SSURGO soil data, and crop cultivation data to build a DSSAT crop model, and transformed the outputs into interactive geospatial layers. This ArcGIS Online web app allows users to explore sweet sorghum suitability patterns across Northwest Minnesota to support long-term bioenergy planning.

Trail Planning & Access

North Country Trail Planning Map

Class Project • 2025

For the North Country Trail Association, I co-managed a team of 18 students to design a route planning model that consolidates current trails, land ownership, easements, and environmental constraints into a single new proposed trail route for a portion of the Minnesota North Country Tail.
